🐔 Chicken Chaos

Race against time and other players to collect corn, avoid obstacles, and spit your way to victory!

🎮 Controls & Objective

  • 🖱️ Move: Move your mouse
  • 🖱️ Click: Spit (max 10 per round)
  • 💥 Spit hits: Stuns chickens for 2s and gives 1s invincibility
  • 🌽 Collect Corn:
    • Yellow = +1 point
    • Pink = +5 points
    • Blue = +3 spit ammo
  • ⏱️ Game Time: 2 minutes or ends when all corn is gone
Time Remaining: 120
Spits Left: 10

🕹️ Game Guide

Move with your mouse • Click to spit

● Yellow Corn = +1   ● Pink Corn = +5   ● Blue Corn = +3 spits

💥 Hitting another chicken stuns them for 2 seconds and gives them 1s invincibility.

Game ends after 2 minutes or when all corn is collected!
